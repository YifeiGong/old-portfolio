Overview

The Goal

It is demonstrably the case that once false information is encountered, it is difficult to remove from peopleâ€™s mindset. Even if the information is corrected with later correct information, it is difficult to extinguish.

If false information is corrected in the moment however, there is a greater chance that it will be corrected in the mind of the reader/viewer.

The Challenge

The challenge would be to provide immediate access to the facts in real or semi-real time.

The Outcome

The goal would be to provide factual verification of the news as it is presented that supports further questions about the context (history, alternative points of view, evidence, etc.) of a news story and/or quote. animation
