Product and UX/UI Designer

Faye dived into the design field in an art high school. She gradually found the design is not only about form and function, but closely related to business, engineering. So she chose to study Industrial Design(B.E.) and got a dual bachelor degree in Economics.

She started her career as an interaction designer and front-end experience product manager and realized uncovering and defining user's needs are more important. That's why she is pursuing a Master study at Northwestern University.

Now, she is focusing on designing and delivering a delightful experience on News and facts, healthcare, and wellness.
